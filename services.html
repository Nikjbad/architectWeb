Common Questions

Service FAQs

What is your design process? ▼

Our process includes five stages: Discovery (understanding requirements), Concept Development (creating ideas), Design Refinement (detailed planning), Planning Documentation (technical drawings), and Project Execution (site management). Each stage involves close client collaboration.

How long does a typical project take? ▼

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Residential design: 3-6 months. Commercial projects: 6-12 months. Construction administration varies with project size. We provide detailed timelines during consultations.

Do you provide sustainable design solutions? ▼

Yes, sustainability is integral to our design philosophy. We incorporate green building practices, energy-efficient systems, sustainable materials, and environmentally responsible construction methods in all projects.

What is your fee structure? ▼

We offer flexible fee structures including fixed fees, percentage-based fees, and hourly rates depending on project type. We discuss and agree on fees transparently during initial consultations.

Do you handle permits and approvals? ▼

Yes, we manage the entire approval process including design consultations with authorities, permit applications, and compliance with local building codes and regulations.

Can you work with existing structures? ▼

Absolutely. We specialize in renovations, adaptations, and expansions of existing buildings, bringing new life to structures while preserving their character and value.
