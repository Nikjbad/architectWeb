Our Story

Award-Winning Architecture For Modern Living

Designing with Purpose

SG Architects is a multidisciplinary design firm founded by Architect Sparsh Gupta in 2009. We are dedicated to transforming architectural visions into extraordinary experiences. From intimate residential projects to landmark commercial developments, every project embodies our commitment to excellence, sustainability, and innovation.

Our Philosophy

We believe architecture is a dialogue between structure and soul—a conversation that should inspire for generations to come. Each project is approached as an opportunity to create spaces that not only meet functional requirements but also elevate the quality of life.

Our Values

  • Design Excellence & Innovation
  • Sustainable & Responsible Building
  • Client-Centric Approach
  • Attention to Detail
  • Timely Delivery & Quality
  • Long-term Client Relationships

Our Specialization

Areas of Expertise

We specialize in creating transformative architectural solutions across multiple disciplines.

Residential Design

Luxury villas, apartments, and residential complexes designed around how clients truly want to live. From concept to completion, we create homes that combine comfort, aesthetics, and functionality.

Commercial Architecture

Office buildings, retail spaces, and mixed-use developments built to perform and inspire. We design commercial spaces that reflect brand identity while maximizing operational efficiency.

Interior Design

Luxury interiors where comfort meets visual poetry. Every element is carefully curated to create cohesive, beautiful spaces that reflect the client's personality and lifestyle.

Urban Planning

Strategic city planning focused on sustainable, liveable communities. We design urban spaces that foster connection, promote well-being, and create vibrant neighborhoods.

Landscape Design

Seamless outdoor environments where natural beauty and architecture become one. From public parks to private gardens, we create outdoor spaces that enhance and inspire.

3D Visualization

Advanced visualization techniques bringing architectural visions to life before construction. Our detailed renderings help clients understand and refine designs.
