Designing with Purpose

SG Architects is a multidisciplinary design firm founded by Architect Sparsh Gupta in 2009. We are dedicated to transforming architectural visions into extraordinary experiences. From intimate residential projects to landmark commercial developments, every project embodies our commitment to excellence, sustainability, and innovation.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our design process, services, and project timelines.

What is your design process? ▼

Our design process includes five stages: Discovery (understanding requirements), Concept Development (creating innovative ideas), Design Refinement (detailed planning), Planning Documentation (technical drawings), and Project Execution (site management and completion).

How long does a typical project take? ▼

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A residential design typically takes 3-6 months, commercial projects 6-12 months, and construction administration varies with project size. We provide detailed timelines during initial consultations.

Do you provide sustainable design solutions? ▼

Yes, sustainability is integral to our design philosophy. We incorporate green building practices, energy-efficient systems, sustainable materials, and environmentally responsible construction methods in all our projects.

What is your fee structure? ▼

We offer flexible fee structures including fixed fees, percentage-based fees, and hourly rates depending on project type and client preference. We discuss and agree on fees transparently during initial consultations.

Do you handle permits and approvals? ▼

Yes, we manage the entire approval process including design consultations with authorities, permit applications, and compliance with local building codes and regulations.

Can you work with existing structures? ▼

Absolutely. We specialize in renovations, adaptations, and expansions of existing buildings, bringing new life to structures while preserving their character and value.

How do you handle budget constraints? ▼

We work closely with clients to understand budget parameters and design accordingly. Our team prioritizes spending on elements that provide maximum value and impact while maintaining design integrity.

What areas do you serve? ▼

We serve clients across India and internationally. Our team has experience working on projects in diverse geographic locations and cultural contexts, ensuring designs are contextually appropriate.
